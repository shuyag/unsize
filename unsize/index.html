Unsize sprouted from the idea that everyone has the right to find a fit that makes them feel good.

Throughout the years, the ready to wear clothing industry has asked us to “fit in, to stand out” in order to love ourselves, but it has never consistently guaranteed that clothes will fit the way they were meant to. In an age where we can customize almost anything, agile manufacturing has yet to become accessible to the average consumer in the fashion industry, and it is not for lack of demand.

In a market space of 30 million bodies to clothe and a 2 billion dollar market opportunity size, unsize uses the intersection of technology and design to provide immediate services to find customers clothes that fit and feel good
